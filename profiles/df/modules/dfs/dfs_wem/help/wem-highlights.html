Usage Scenario:
Great for a quick run­though at a tradeshow/conference or an initial in­person/WebEx meeting for business users interested in WEM. Highlights the web experience for an end user across the customer journey of content community and commerce.
Persona:
End user/consumer
What the Demo Covers:
  • High level overview of content, community and commerce capabilities
  • Shows the power of Drupal’s architecture, leverages metadata and events to deliver the right content to the right person at the right time
  • End user experience on a Drupal site, highlighting Drupal’s ability to automatically personalize content based on a user’s stated personal attributes (their interests, skills, etc.)
  • Shows native community capabilities. The user is invited to join the contest and become a more loyal brand advocate, and sees what their peers are engaged
  • Demonstrates a transformative, content­rich shopping experience for the end user and the ability to add user­generated content and share that content to popular social media sites.
  • Shows the ability to integrate owned social networks, commerce, public social networks and rich content into a single platform
